CASE STUDY

Neolearn Rollout

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Deploying an indigenous e-learning platform for multi-organization training.

Neolifeporium designed and implemented a practical learning ecosystem for institutional teams requiring structured upskilling at scale. The solution integrated content delivery, learner management, and reporting workflows.

Client Info

Sector: Multi-sector organizations in Ghana

Services: Neolearn setup, digital infrastructure, change enablement

Duration: 6 months phased rollout

CHALLENGE

Fragmented training delivery, poor visibility into learner progress, and inconsistent digital accessibility across distributed teams.

SOLUTION

A modular Neolearn implementation with role-based content paths, centralized analytics, and simplified onboarding for administrators and learners.
