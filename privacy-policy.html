Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026. This policy explains how Neolifeporium Limited collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use our website and business services.

Introduction

Neolifeporium Limited is a Ghanaian business services provider and corporate branch of Gazoma Pay. We are committed to handling personal data transparently and responsibly.

Data Collection

Information You Provide

We may collect your name, business contact information, email address, and any details you provide through forms, direct communication, or service onboarding.

Technical Information

We may collect browser data, device type, IP address, and usage signals to improve website performance and reliability.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for website operation. Optional cookies for analytics, preferences, and marketing are controlled through our consent settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party platforms for hosting, communications, analytics, and service delivery. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and contractual obligations.

Data Security

We apply organizational and technical controls to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

User Rights

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion where legally and operationally applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for optional cookie categories.

Contact Information

For privacy requests, contact: hello@neolifeporium.com.
